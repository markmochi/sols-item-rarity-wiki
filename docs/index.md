    <div class="guide-step__symbol">×2</div>
    <strong>Read the roll</strong>
    <span>Higher rarity scales its configured equipment bonus.</span>
  </div>
  <div class="guide-step">
    <b class="guide-step__number">04</b>
    <img src="assets/images/items/diamond_pickaxe.png" alt="Diamond pickaxe">
    <strong>Keep the best</strong>
    <span>Tools can also roll unique perks and perk tiers.</span>
  </div>
</div>

The roll belongs to that item. Dropping, storing, trading, or moving it does not roll it again. If you want a different rarity or a different tool-perk set, you need another fresh item.

!!! tip "The short answer"
    There is no player reroll, leveling, or ascension system. Progress comes from finding another copy with a stronger rarity or better utility perks.
